Title: Ethos Food Trading
----
Category: Visual Identity
----
Client: Ethos Food Trading
----
Summary: Ethos Food Trading is a company launched with the purpose of delivering the finest and most nutritious natural products from Greece and Cyprus, free from harmful pesticides and inherently rich in flavour. Ethos Food Trading are committed to sourcing products from local farmers who engage in sustainable and GMO-free farming.

The visual identity centres around the Flower of Life; an ancient form of sacred geometry comprised of concentric circles in a hexagonal configuration. It symbolises the origin of all life, the harmonious connection between all living things, health and longevity, and the cyclic nature of time.
